About

The Tim Lee Roadshow prides itself on being a quality mobile disco. I offer a professional and personal experience. I provide entertainments to a wide range of events including but not exclusive to weddings, birthday parties, Christmas parties, corporate events and themed nights. I have in my collection a wide range of music dating from the swinging 60’s to the present. My equipment provides any event with top quality sounds and lighting creating an unforgettable atmosphere. I have a variety of sets from a retro disco, with light boxes and large mirror ball to a more modern show with S&H DJ L.E.D booth and uplighting. Alongside providing you with your entertainment needs I am happy to announce raffles, present prizes and make announcements throughout your event including introducing other entertainment acts you may have. I have current public liability insurance and all of my equipment is PAT tested regularly to ensure safety and quality for your event. I am based in Wisbech. I cover areas up to and including Kings Lynn, Downham Market, Hunstanton, Peterborough, Stamford and Ely. I am happy to discuss any event enquiry so please do not hesitate to get in touch.

I have been a DJ for over 25 years. I started back in 1989 in my hometown of Kings Lynn using vinyl records. I was hooked after helping a very successful disco for 3 years before starting the Tim Lee Roadshow after that I have never looked back. I strongly believe from your first inquiry to my performance on the evening of your event that you should receive a professional and personal experience which is why you will only ever deal with me for all of your entertainment needs.

I received many calls last year from people who had been let down at the last minute by other mobile disco companies. If you find yourself in this situation and choose me for your event needs, you will not be charged late booking fees or find unwelcome hidden extra’s. My company is now based in Wisbech. In all my years as a mobile disco provider Have never cancelled a booking with a client All our dates and bookings are confirmed with a signed contract. We take requests during the evening, you can also submit a playlist before the event which we will incorporate during an event. We are registered with the Telephone Preference Agency and do not accept marketing calls.

Services

Weddings

I have played at many wedding receptions hosted at a variety of different venues. This enables me to provide your perfect day with perfectly tailored entertainment guaranteed to have your guests on their feet all night. Weddings are remembered fondly by all involved, with shared memories of a great day. The Tim Lee Roadshow aims to help make those memories, with forgotten classics, mixed with up to the minute requests, allowing all generations to mix it up on the dance floor of your day. I take pride in providing quality entertainment centred around the happy couple. I like to meet you at least once to ensure that all your needs and requirements for your big day are met allowing things to run as smoothly as possible for your perfect day. Your booking with The Tim Lee Roadshow will be confirmed in writing and will include the contract and pricing agreement. The Tim Lee Roadshow does not take any responsibility for the quantity or quality of the ‘Dad Dancing’.

Uplighting

One of the best ways to transform a room and create a “magical atmosphere" is to use specialist lighting effects which "colour wash" the floor, ceiling and walls. I am delighted to be able to offer you this additional service when you book with The Tim Lee Roadshow. All of the lighting effects are specifically tailored to match your decorative requirements. I can meet you at the venue to discuss what you want aswell as what I need to do and provide you with visual examples. The Time Lee Roadshow create stunning visual effects, using a series of static lights positioned around the room. These can be programmed to perform instant colour transitions or more subtle fade in and fade out effects. I am confident that you won't be disappointed with the results. I will work with your idea’s to achieve your perfect lighting needs. I use par cans, linked and controlled by a central DMX light controller.
